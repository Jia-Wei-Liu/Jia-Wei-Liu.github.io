Hello! I am a Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Lab, ex-FAIR. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science from National University of Singapore, advised by Prof. Mike Zheng Shou and Prof. Jussi Keppo. I am working on Multi-Media 3D/4D/Video Generation and Perception.
